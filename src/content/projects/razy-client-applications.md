## Overview

During my three years at Razy Technology, I worked as a full-stack developer delivering a wide range of B2B web applications. This phase of my career gave me broad exposure to different industries and taught me to adapt quickly to new business domains.

**Key Projects:**

**Customer Loyalty Platforms (Majesty, Bticino, Encreez)**
Multi-tenant loyalty management systems with point tracking, reward catalogs, and customer analytics.

**Dentalore — Dental Practice Management**
Complete dental practice management: patient records, appointment scheduling, treatment plans, and billing with insurance claim generation.

**SOMA — Automotive Service Platform**
Vehicle inspection tracking, service history, and customer communication portal for automotive workshops.

**Document Archiving System**
Enterprise document management with full-text search, version control, and access permissions.

## Technical Stack

All applications built on ASP.NET MVC 5 / Web API with SQL Server backends. Used Kendo UI for rich data grids and forms, Telerik Reporting for PDF generation, Firebase for real-time notifications, and SignalR for live dashboard updates.
